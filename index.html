    INTERNATIONAL HARD ANODIZING ASSOCIATION

    Connecting People & Solutions in Hard Anodizing

    Founded in 1989, the International Hard Anodizing Association is a community of aluminum finishers from around the world whose core competence is the production of hard anodized surfaces on aluminum components. The group also includes “associate” members - manufacturers of semi-finished aluminum products, suppliers of chemicals, processes or plant equipment to the hard anodizing industry, as well as companies that use hard anodized components. The IHHA’s mission is to foster the global exchange of knowledge, technical assistance between members and collaboration in the definition of standards. The association thrives on the social connections of its members and looks forward to welcoming each new colleague.

    In an industry as specialized as hard anodizing, opportunities to learn from the examples and experiences of other professionals are rare. That’s what makes the free exchange of ideas within the IHAA so valuable. Membership provides you with the unique opportunity to regularly share perspectives with other men and women who are shaping the future of the industry. Alongside tours of their plants and symposiums on best practices in anodizing, you’ll find colleagues who understand the intricacies and challenges of your profession.

    The IHAA Quality Seal

    The IHAA Quality Seal is the only one of its kind in the world issued by a non-profit organization. All necessary services are performed by the IHAA on a purely voluntary basis and only cost recovery must be achieved. The goal of the IHAA Seal is to recognize the truly best, most innovative and leading anodizing companies in the world and not to issue the largest possible number of certifications in order to make the highest possible profits. We want to achieve a clear separation of the absolute top companies from the rest!
